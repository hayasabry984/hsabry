Yoga

The workout plan

Give your brain a break and achieve inner peace with this beginner-friendly mind and body relaxation sequence. These 12 soothing yoga poses will help you redirect your mind to become conscious of our breath and allow every muscle in your body to be in a relaxed state of awareness.

Her pose: 60 sec
Description: Kneel on the floorwith your knees together and your feet hip-width apart. Sit back between your feet, place your hands on your thighs and gaze forword.

Extended puppy pose: 60 sec
Description: Walk your hands forword, pull your hips back toward the heels and lengthen the spine. Rest your forehead on the mat and relax.

Lion pose: 30 sec
Description:Bring your feet together, open the knees and place your hands on the floor with your fingers pointnig back.

Cat pose: 15 sec + 15 sec + 15 sec
Description: Get on yours hands and knees and exhale as you round your spin towards the celling. Repeat this 3 times.

Cow pose: 15 sec + 15 sec + 15 sec
Description: Inhale as you curve your belly down towards the floor and feel the stretch in your chest and neck. Repeat this 3 times.

Downword facing dog pose: 30 sec
Description: Extend your arms and legs and lift your hips up and back. Breathe deeply, open your shoulders and push your heels down.

Standing forword bend pose: 30 sec
Description: Exhale as you walk your back, draw your belly in and lengthen your torso.

Upward salute pose: 30 sec
Description: Inhale as you left your torso and brings your arms up and overhead. Expand the chest, look up and mentain your neck and head relaxed.

Warrior pose: 30 sec + 30 sec
Description: Step to the side with your left foot and rotate it out, to a 90-degree angel, bend your left knee, keep it in line with the ankle, rotate your head to the left and bring the arms up to shoulder height.

Tree pose: 30 sec + 30 sec
Description: Transfer your weight to the right foot, bend your left knee, and place the sole of your left foot against the inner right thigh. Inhal as you raise your arms above your head and press your hands together.

Seated forward bend pose: 30 sec
Description: Set on the floor with your feet together and legs extended. Exhale as you bend at the hips and slowly lower your torso.

Reclining bound angel pose: 60 sec
Description: Lie down in the mat, bend your knees and let your thighs fall open. Press the soles of your feet together and keep your knees relaxed.

The workout plan

Stretch the entire front and back of the body, build strength and boost your energy levels with this 20-minute full body energizing flow. A morning yoga routine that gives you the amount of stretch and focus you need to have a calm and productive day.

Mountain pose: 30 sec
Description: Stand up tall with your feet together and distribute your weight equally between both feet. Keep your spine along, your body soft and bring your palme together in front of your heart.

Upward salute pose: 30 sec
Description: Inhale as you left your torso and brings your arms up and overhead. Expand the chest, look up and mentain your neck and head relaxed.

Standing forword bend pose: 30 sec
Description: Exhale as you walk your back, draw your belly in and lengthen your torso.

High lunge pose: 30 sec
Description: Inhale, step your left leg straight back and lay your torso on the right thigh. Step your left leg back on the first and third set and step the right leg back on the second and fourth set.

Low lunge pose: 30 sec
Description: Exhale lower your left knee to the floor and place the top on the left foot on the floor. Inhale, lift your torso up and sweep your arms out to the sides and overhead.

Plank pose: 30 sec
Description: Exhale, place your hands on the floor and step your right leg back beside the left.

Upword facing dog pose: 30 sec
Description: Inhale, lengthen the spine and bring your arms up and overhead.
